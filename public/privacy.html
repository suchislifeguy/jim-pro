Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 10, 2026

JIM ("we", "us", or "our") provides a job management platform for tradespeople. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a better service to all our users. This includes:

  • Business Profile: Your business name, ABN/registration number, address, phone number, and logo.
  • Customer Data: Information you enter about your clients, including names, addresses, phone numbers, and job details.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, such as features accessed and time spent in the app.
  • AI Prompts: Text and images you provide to our AI assistant (JIM) for processing.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the JIM application.
  • Generate quotes, invoices, and reports for your business.
  • Process AI requests (e.g., professionalizing text or scanning receipts).
  • Manage your subscription and payments via Stripe.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in two primary locations:

  • Local Storage: Most of your job data is stored directly on your device using IndexedDB and LocalStorage. This allows the app to work offline.
  • Cloud Storage: If you use sync features, your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore and Cloud Storage).

4. AI and Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may process your data:

  • Google Firebase: For data hosting, authentication, and database services.
  • Google Gemini API: AI prompts and images are sent to Google for processing. We do not store the content of your AI prompts on our own servers beyond what is necessary to process the request.
  • Stripe: Payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe. We do not store your credit card details.

5. Your Rights and Data Deletion

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. You can delete your data directly within the app settings. If you have a registered account, you can request full account deletion by contacting us.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@jim-pro.app
